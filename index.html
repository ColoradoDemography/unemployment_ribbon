State Demography Office - Unemployment Rate Analysis

This application shows the three periods of unemployment rate data by month for a selected county for three time periods:

  • The Current Year,
  • The Previous Year, and
  • A summary ribbon showing the range in the unemployment rate for either
    • The past five years, or
    • The period of the prior unemployment peak (2009 to 2012)
